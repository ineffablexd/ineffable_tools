It handles complex coordinate transformations to ensure valid rendering of map extents, grids (WGS84), and scale bars. Key features include adaptive scale units (switches between meters and km), dynamic "North" labels, automated legend synchronization, and a sleek progress-tracking UI. Developed to streamline cartographic workflows for GIS professionals, ensuring stable and aesthetically pleasing map outputs every time.
